# Grit and Growth Mindset

## Question 1
### Paraphrase the Grit video in a few lines.

Grit means continuing to work towards a goal even when things become difficult. Talent alone is not enough. Consistent effort and persistence over a long time matter more.

---

## Question 2
### Paraphrase the Growth Mindset video in a few lines.

A growth mindset means believing that skills and intelligence can improve with learning and practice. Mistakes and failures are part of the learning process.

---

## Question 3
### What is the Internal Locus of Control? What is the key point in the video?

Internal locus of control means believing that our actions and decisions influence our results. The key point in the video was to focus more on things we can control instead of blaming outside situations.

---

## Question 4
### What are the key points mentioned by the speaker to build growth mindset?

- Accept challenges
- Learn from failures
- Stop fearing mistakes
- Focus on improvement
- Practice consistently
- Be open to feedback
- Avoid comparing with others
- Replace “I can’t” with “I can learn”
- Stay curious
- Keep learning continuously
