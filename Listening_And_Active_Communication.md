# Listening and Active Communication

## Question 1
### What are the steps/strategies to do Active Listening?

- Give full attention to the speaker
- Maintain eye contact
- Do not interrupt while the other person is speaking
- Listen to understand, not just to reply
- Observe body language and tone
- Ask questions if something is unclear
- Repeat or summarize important points
- Take notes wherever necessary
- Show interest through nodding or short responses

---

## Question 2
### According to Fisher's model, what are the key points of Reflective Listening?

- Listen carefully to what the other person is saying
- Understand both words and emotions
- Repeat the message in your own words
- Confirm whether you understood correctly
- Avoid judging or interrupting
- Make the speaker feel heard and understood

---

## Question 3
### What are the obstacles in your listening process?

- Thinking about my reply before the other person finishes
- Getting distracted easily
- Sometimes assuming I already understood the point

---

## Question 4
### What can you do to improve your listening?

- Keep distractions away during conversations
- Focus completely on the speaker
- Take notes in important discussions
- Ask questions when confused
- Avoid interrupting people
- Try to understand the message properly before replying

---

## Question 5
### When do you switch to Passive communication style in your day to day life?

- In situations where I want to avoid conflict
- Sometimes in group discussions when I am unsure

---

## Question 6
### When do you switch into Aggressive communication styles in your day to day life?

- During stressful situations or deadlines
- When I feel strongly about something

---

## Question 7
### When do you switch into Passive Aggressive communication styles in your day to day life?

- When I get annoyed but do not say it directly
- When I avoid discussing a problem openly

---

## Question 8
### How can you make your communication assertive?

- Speak clearly and directly
- Express opinions respectfully
- Listen carefully before responding
- Maintain calm body language and tone
- Say no politely when needed
- Avoid blaming or insulting others
- Be confident while speaking
- Focus on solving the problem instead of arguing
