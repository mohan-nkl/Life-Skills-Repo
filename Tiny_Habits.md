## Question 2
### How can you use B = MAP to make making new habits easier? What are M, A and P?

- B = Behavior
- M = Motivation
- A = Ability
- P = Prompt

A habit becomes easier when we increase ability by making the task simple, use prompts as reminders, and avoid depending only on motivation.

---

## Question 3
### Why is it important to "Shine" or Celebrate after each successful completion of habit?

Celebrating makes the brain connect the habit with positive feelings. When we feel good after doing something, the brain wants to repeat it again, which helps the habit become permanent.

## Question 6
### Write about the book's perspective on how to make a habit easier to do.

- Start with very small actions
- Reduce friction
- Keep required things ready beforehand
- Make the habit simple and convenient
- Create a fixed routine and environment

---

## Question 7
### Write about the book's perspective on how to make a habit harder to do.

- Increase friction
- Remove triggers or distractions
- Make the habit inconvenient
- Add extra steps before doing it
- Avoid environments that encourage the habit

---

## Question 8
### Pick one habit that you would like to do more of. What are the steps you can take?

Habit: Walking daily and eating healthy food

- Keep fixed walking timings every day
- Keep walking shoes ready and visible
- Start even if it is only a short walk
- Prepare healthy food in advance whenever possible
- Avoid skipping meals and then overeating later
- Track daily walks and healthy eating consistently
- Feel good after completing the habit instead of treating it like pressure

---

## Question 9
### Pick one habit that you would like to eliminate or do less of. What are the steps you can take?

Habit: Eating junk food frequently

- Avoid keeping junk food nearby
- Do not order food when bored or stressed
- Replace junk food with healthier snacks
- Drink water first before craving snacks
- Avoid food delivery apps during late nights
- Remind myself how I feel after overeating junk food
- Make healthy food easier to access than junk food
