# Focus Management

## Question 1
### What is Deep Work?

Deep work means working with complete focus on an important task without distractions for a long period of time.

---

## Question 2
### According to the author how to do deep work properly?

- Remove distractions before starting work
- Schedule dedicated deep work time
- Focus on one task at a time
- Avoid constant social media and notifications
- Train the mind to concentrate deeply
- Take proper breaks after focused work
- Prioritize important work over shallow tasks

---

## Question 3
### How can you implement the principles in your day to day life?

- Keep phone on silent mode while working and learning 
- Avoid checking social media frequently
- Turn off notifications or use Zen mode
- Allocate fixed hours for focused work 
- Work on one task instead of multitasking
- Take short breaks between intense sessions
- Go for walks or sit quietly to refresh the mind
- Do not take reward before finishing the work

---

## Question 4
### What are the dangers of social media, in brief?

- Reduces attention span
- Creates addiction and distraction
- Wastes a lot of time without realizing
- Increases comparison and anxiety
- Reduces ability to focus deeply
- Affects sleep and mental peace
- Makes people dependent on constant stimulation
- Affects social relationships
- May cause brain fog
